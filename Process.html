Process of hot and cold mounting of specimen

Hot mounting :

* Process to be followed while placing the specimen.

* Switch on the power button .

* Apply antistick stearate powder to the surface of lower ram and upper ram to avoid sticking of resin.

* Place the specimen on the ram with the surface to be viewed facing downdards.

* Pouring resin over the specimen.

* Press and hold the RAM DOWN to lower the ram to it's lower limit.

* Fill asuitable amount of resin about 1 big scoop into the cylinder through the funnel.

* Installing the top closure.

* Place top closure with the upper ram on the mounting cylinder.

* Place the top closure downand turn it clockwise until it's lower limit.

* Turn the top closure a three quarters of a turn back.

* Parameters before starting the mounting process.

* Check the table for correct resin specifications eg:heating time ;cooling time.

* Press start the process will run automatically .

* The machine automatically stops and relives the pressure when the cooling time has elapsed.

* Removing the top closure.

* Turn top closure in counter clock-wise direction until released from the thread.

* Hold the top closure and press RAM up to raise the lower RAM to it's upper limit.

* Remove the sample .

* Switch off the hot mount press.

COLD MOUNTING:

* Switch on compresso.r

* Insert tubing into the stopper ( ensure enough length for the epoxy to flow into the module).

* Place sample in the mould and place them inside chamber.

* Turn the vacuum knob clock wise to max.

* Turn the knob behind the Epovac to the anticlockwise to pump down the chamber.

* Place tubing on the metal stand and tighten the knob.

* Tighten stopper and knobto pump down to reach about 400m bar .

* Allow the vacuum to hold for about 5 minutes.

* Prepare epoxy compound. Get a disposable cup.

* Fill syringe with 20ml of epoxy resin. Inject 20ml of resin into mixing cup.

* Inject 5ml of hardner into cup and stirr slowly about 5 minutes to reduce the formation of bubbles.

* Place tubing into the cup and release the knob to allow epoxy to flow.

* Try not to release any bubbles into the mould.

* After sufficient epoxy is added, tighten the knob to stop the flow of epoxy.

*Turn the vacuum knob to min and wait for 30 minutes.

* vent the chamber by turning the knob behind the epovac clockwise.

* Remove the mould with the sample inside and leave it to harden for 8 hours before removing the cold mount from the mould.
